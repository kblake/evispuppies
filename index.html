Evi's Aussie Doodle Puppies

They are due on Christmas Day!

About the puppies

  • They will be F1B mini-medium Aussiedoodles
  • Non-shedding
  • The average full grown weight of each puppy will be about 30 pounds which is considered a mini Aussiedoodle, however, they could be as big as mama (40#) or as small as dad (19#)
  • A wide range of beautiful colors on their coat which makes them unique to other dog breeds
  • Highly intelligent and loyal and make for good family dogs

They will be $2000 each and will ready to go home around 8 weeks of age.

A deposit will hold a puppy for you. Please inquire at ask@evisaussiedoodles.dog.

About the mama: Evi is a wonderful family dog. She is friendly, intelligent and gets along well with others. She loves playtime and is very affectionate as she cuddles with everyone in the family. She enjoys any activities our family does, including hiking, swimming and running. She is an F1 Aussiedoodle, is 40 pounds, and has a non-shedding bicolored brown and white coat. About the dad: Mr Wooster is friendly and gets along with everyone. He has a gentle spirit. He is a miniature poodle, 19 pounds, AKC registered and a deep red color.
